Amass (virtual) riches every round by buying and selling words on twitter.

Words are priced based on how often they're mentioned on twitter. A word that costs $10 has been mentioned in one percent of all recent tweets.

So how to win? Predict what's going to be hot, buy it up ("pretweet it!") before it hits twitter, and you'll make a killing once people start talking about it.
